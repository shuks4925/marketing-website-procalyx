TYPE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

When registering and creating an account, the Users may be required to provide personal, non-personal, and other information, as indicated below (such information and correspondences hereinafter collectively referred to as "Information"):

User Information. When you register on our Platform, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and organization details. Inventory and Transaction Data, including inventory requirements submitted by hospitals, such as product specifications, quantities, and procurement schedules. Pharmaceutical companies may provide quotations, pricing details, stock availability, regulatory documents, and purchase terms. Hospital Data. All information provided by hospitals on the Platform, including but not limited to medicine usage, requirements, procurement patterns, and inventory levels.

Usage Data. We track interactions with the Platform, including browsing behavior, product searches, time spent on different sections, and preferences to enhance user experience.

Non-Personal Information. We collect non-personal information during Platform visits, including domain, host, IP address, operating system, browser type, cookies, and internet service provider. This information is not personally identifiable.

Permitted Information. Where you opt to provide any device permissions, such as contact list, photos, camera, location, microphone, SMS, storage, and phone calls, the Company may retrieve, retain, and use the data sourced through such device permissions.

Automatic Information. For enhanced Platform responsiveness, we utilize cookies and electronic tools to gather data on individual interests. This includes but is not limited to the server logs with details like web requests, IP addresses, browser type, language, date, time, and unique browser identifiers, primarily for user authentication.

Mobile. When accessing the Platform via mobile, we may obtain location and device information, using it to provide service-related suggestions if needed.

Communications and Other Data. We retain emails, enquiries, feedback, survey responses, logs, and optional information (such as social background or affirmative action eligibility), including details exchanged via linked social media accounts.
